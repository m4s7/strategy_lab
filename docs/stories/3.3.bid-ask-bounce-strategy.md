## Dev Notes
This story implements a Level 1 data-based strategy that contrasts with the Level 2 order book imbalance strategy, demonstrating framework versatility.

**Strategy Implementation Context:**
- **Module**: src/strategy_lab/strategies/implementations/bid_ask_bounce.py
- **Base Class**: Inherits from StrategyBase
- **Data Requirements**: Level 1 tick data (trades, best bid/ask)
- **Strategy Type**: Mean reversion scalping strategy

**Bid-Ask Bounce Algorithm:**
- **Bounce Detection**: Identify when price touches bid/ask and reverses
- **Strength Measurement**: Calculate bounce magnitude and velocity
- **Pattern Recognition**: Distinguish true bounces from trend continuation
- **Market Microstructure**: Use spread and volume as confirmation

**Entry Logic Design:**
- **Long Entry**: Price touches bid and shows upward movement
- **Short Entry**: Price touches ask and shows downward movement
- **Confirmation**: Require minimum bounce strength and volume
- **Timing**: Enter on momentum confirmation, not just touch

**Exit Strategy Components:**
- **Profit Targets**: Fixed tick targets or percentage-based
- **Stop Losses**: Opposite side of spread plus buffer
- **Time Exits**: Maximum holding period to avoid overnight risk
- **Momentum Exits**: Exit when bounce momentum fades

**Market Condition Filters:**
- **Spread Filter**: Avoid trading when spreads are too wide
- **Volatility Filter**: Reduce position size in high volatility
- **Volume Filter**: Require minimum volume for signal validity
- **Session Filter**: Avoid illiquid periods and session transitions

**Configuration Parameters:**
```yaml
strategy:
  name: "bid_ask_bounce"
  parameters:
    bounce_sensitivity: 0.7
    min_bounce_strength: 0.5
    profit_target_ticks: 2
    stop_loss_ticks: 1
    max_spread_ticks: 2
    min_volume: 10
    max_holding_seconds: 120
```

**Performance Tracking:**
- **Bounce Success Rate**: Percentage of detected bounces that follow through
- **Entry Timing**: How quickly after bounce detection entry occurs
- **Exit Effectiveness**: Profit target vs stop loss hit rates
- **Market Condition Impact**: Performance across different market states
